Fund operation and strategy

This page presents what Alpha Centauri invests in, and what is the fund management strategy.

Strategic investing and trading

Fund structure

The new Fund structure comprising three components allows for meaningful diversification of income streams and compartmentalization of risks. In this manner the Fund can continuously generate profit, in different economic periods.

Trading account

The trading account is used to generate ultra-short term profits from changes in gold and silver spot prices. At the moment, these average out to 500 $ a week.

Physical gold reserves

Holding a substantial portion of wealth in the form of gold is never a bad idea. Gold bars maybe don`t generate profit, however, there is no c no counter-party risk, and it is an excellent hedge against depreciation of any currency.


General Fund structure

After December 2025 major revision of the strategy, weighing out the current global risks and opportunities the Fund now has three components, as outlined below.

Alpha Centauri A (aC A)

The trading account is the main generator of profit through ultra-short term trades (up to 1 week). It is currently capped at 10 000 $ for risk management reasons. As the experience and skill of the Manager increases, so does the efficiency of usage of capital. Hence, this amount of money is sufficient for maintenance of steady profit. Any profits above the 10K threshold will be used to fill other components of the Fund.

Alpha Centauri B (aC B)

The Manager has decided to increase gold reserves from earlier 20% to planned about a third. Due to the long-term economic trend of depreciation of paper currencies, gold price is expected to rise in the next years. Ample gold reserves mean that the Fund is "long" gold at all time.


Proxima Centauri (Proxima)

This component is currently being filled. Here the money is directed towards fixed income instruments (governmental bonds). Proxima currently serves as a relatively safe and slightly growing instrument where money is kept until a good investment opportunity comes along.

Day-to-day operation

Incoming money from Investors will be used among the Fund components according to the allocation needs of the Fund at that moment, but will be treated as a part of common money pool of entire Fund level. Likewise, any withdrawal of Investors will be considered as coming out of the common money pool, irrespective of status of Fund components.


At the moment, winter 2025, the fund generates profit in aC A component by ultra-short trades on gold and silver spot prices. The generated profit is periodically re-distributed among longer-term investments in aC B and Proxima.


The Manager has concucted, tested and written down several trading strategies that are applicable to gold and silver markets. These tactics each have defined risks and earning potential, and the Manager employs either tactic based on current price action, situation on commodities markets and general sentiment of market participants.


Longer-term outlook

The Fund targets at least 5% monthly profit, relative to the total Fund size. Inevitably, losing trades will occur, either due to bad decisions or due to change of overall trading strategy. In these cases, the Manager will offset losing positions and close them in a controlled manner so that a stable overall monthly profit is maintained.


The Manager may take on more credit to boost the trading account size when the conditios are favourable. In this case, it will be imperative that the Fund produces significantly more profit than the monthly refund rate of the credit.


Once the operaitng risks are minimizes and the stability and predictability of profits achieved, the Fund will be incorporated. The most likely scenario is a Limited Liability Company with current Alpha Investors converted to shareholders receiving dividends.

Risk management

Subdivision of the Fund into three components, each with its own financial instruments and purpose, and without direct money link, allows for compartmentalization of risks.

A strong rule is that Fund`s own cash + gold reserves have to be bigger than the sum of all investors` cash. This ensures that the Fund stays liquid and will be able to pay out possible withdrawals at any time.

Alpha Centauri does not engage in high-risk activities. The Fund has a target growth rate of 5% monthly. This amount is sufficient for relevant growth of fund sizewithout engaging in too much risk.

The Manager is at all time alert to emerging and potential risks and pro-actively plans their mitigation. Sets of pre-defined measures are in place in order to mitigate systemic, market and political risks if they would occur.
