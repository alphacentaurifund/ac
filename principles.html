Fund operation and strategy

This page presents what Alpha Centauri invests in, and what is the fund management strategy.

Investment instruments

Alpha Centauri must invest in instruments that allow creation of a consistent strategy. As of September 2024, Alpha Centauri invests in precious metals. Physical gold holdings represent a security insurance, while the Fund makes profits by short-term trading of contracts on silver.

Gold reserves

Gold is by no means outdated. It has had a status of monetary metal for millennia. It provides a robust protection against paper currency depreciation. Physical gold also has no counterparty risk, and can be sold at any time for any currency.

Silver market

Silver commodity has a dual purpose: monetary and industrial metal. All refined silver that is not bought by industry becomes bought by investors, which accummulate significant amounts. Hence, silver market is not influenced by its supply and demand, but by how much investors price it.

Principles of operation


Alpha Centauri has two main components: the trading account and the reserves. Parameters of the trading account are disclosed to the Investors, while the content of reserves is presented just as aggregate fraction.


The trading account is the "work horse" of the Fund, and it is through its activity where profits are made. Reserves can be physical gold, cash in other currencies, or other assets that retain their value at the moment.


The Manager will balance the trading account size and the reserves. Reserves, although necessary, should not take up resources which could otherwise contribute to the profit- making trading account.


New money coming as Investor contributions will go straigh into the trading account to increase its benchmark size.


Both the money of the Manager and of the Investors is combined. Hence, the Manager has an equal interest for the Fund to do well as do the Investors. The stake of the Manager is actually higher because he has more money in total in the Fund.


Alpha Centauri is currently trading prices of silver. According to credible sources of information, this commodity has predictable supply and demand dynamics, and it has a robust demand by investors. Regarding the Fund`s method of profit production, it is not so important where the price is at at the moment, but rather if it can be traded. Silver price has a long-term growth trajectory, and it is volatile enough on a daly basis to bring running profits.


The Manager will keep to the words written here in order to keep the stability of operations, otherwise it would be like gambling for Investors. Nevertheless, the Manager retains the right to modify the general trading target if the macroeconomic conditions change.


Like every undertaking, the performance of Alpha Centauri will inevitably have a wave pattern. During good times the Manager will accummulate reserves, and during hard times he will dose these reserves into the trading account to ensure its continuous operation.


The Fund aims to bring in constant stream of profits and achieve a steady growth. At the moment the strategy consists of taking small positions at financial instruments and taking profits after change in its price. As the Fund grows, other types of more robust strategies will be explored. It is the economy of scale - the more you have, the more you can take.


The Fund is at the moment a private project. The Investors are encouraged to re-invest earned interest instead of withdrawing it, until a corporate structure of the Fund can be established. This will probably come in a year or two, when the running profits will justify taking in an accounting professional (check the tab "Legal and Strategy".
