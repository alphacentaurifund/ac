For Investors

The Investors Programme of Alpha Centauri was launched in August 2024 and will run until the conversion of the Fund into a Limited Liability Company.

Main details about the Investor Programme can be found below, while a full normative document on relationships with Investors can be downloaded here

Interest rate

Annualized interest rate to Investors is 12%.

A 3% interest rate is accorded each 3 months, and compounding.

The Manager can discretionally accord sign-up bonuses or one-time bonuses following exceptionally good trading profits.

Deposit

The Fund accepts new investors by the invitation from the Manager or by a strong recommendation from an existing Investor

The Fund collects deposits at bank accounts in Erste bank Romania or Revolut Bank (Lithuania). Accounts in EUR and USD are active.

Please contact the Manager about deposit details.

Contributions record

The Manager keeps a detailed record of all contributions, including dates, bank accounts, currencies and amounts.

Investors can at any moment request a statement document of their contributions and interest amounts accummulated hitherto.

Withdrawals

Investors have the right to partial or complete withdrawal of their contributions and interest at any time.

Cybersecurity

Alpha Centauri does not take safety in online spaces lightly. Hence, all devices used to log in into trading and banking services are secured with full versions of VPN and antivirus software. Subscriptions are paid annually from the profits and do not affect Investors

Market-wide events

Below are some of the biggest market-moving events from the recent past. Since the Investors in Alpha Centauri receive a fixed interest rate these events do not have an immediate impact. Nevertheless, the investors may still have felt these effects if they have other investments elsewhere.

Yen carry trade unwinding

Aug 2024

Notable drop (up to 10%) across all markets due to Bank of Japan rising its key interest rate.
US elections, Trump

Nov 2024

Trump won the elections, stock market soared, crypto also, and the metals (precious and industrial) became stagnant.
Inflation forecasted by Fed

Dec 2024

At the 18. Jan monetary policy meeting, the Fed left the interest rate unchanged, but forecasted inflation in 2025. This triggered a massive (10 %) sell-off across all markets.
DXY rising

Nov - Dec 2024

The December drop of valuations in most asset classes was also fueled by the unusual increase in US Dollar Index to over 110 points.
Trump inauguration

20. Jan 2025 onwards

President Trump is a showman, but his takeover of the US government seems to have stabilized the markets. Shortly after, the stock and precious metals valuations returned to early Dec 2024 levels.
