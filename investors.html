For investors

Materials for investors and details about Alpha Centauri business.

The Investors Programme of Alpha Centauri was launched in August 2024. The underlying idea was to accelerate the Fund growth with external money and to bring friends and colleagues along on the journey to financial freedom. <

The Fund has a dedicated set of guidelines for new Investors or existing Investors that wish to consult the funds deposition and withdrawal principles.

Open the normative document

Investors are a significant and very inportant pillar of Alpha Centauri. Hence, the Fund will always keep the interest of the Investors in high regard, and any issues will be resolved to the benefit of the Investor.

In contrast to conventional investment funds, the policy of Alpha Centauri guarantees that investors cannot lose money because of trading mistakes. The Fund will support potential losses from its own resources.

Interest rate

Annualized interest rate to Investors is 12%.

A 3% interest rate is accorded each 3 months, and compounding.

The Manager can discretionally accord sign-up bonuses or one-time bonuses following exceptionally good trading profits.

Deposit

The Fund accepts new investors by the invitation from the Manager or by a strong recommendation from an existing Investor

The Fund collects deposits at bank accounts in Erste bank Romania or Revolut Bank (Lithuania). Accounts in EUR and USD are active.

Please contact the Manager about deposit details.

Contributions record

The Manager keeps a detailed record of all contributions, including dates, bank accounts, currencies and amounts.

Investors can at any moment request a statement document of their contributions and interest amounts accummulated hitherto.

Withdrawals

Investors have the right to partial or complete withdrawal of their contributions and interest at any time.

Cybersecurity

Alpha Centauri does not take safety in online spaces lightly. Hence, all devices used to log in into trading and banking services are secured with full versions of VPN and antivirus software. Subscriptions are paid annually from the profits and do not affect Investors
