For Investors

Deposit

The Fund accepts new investors by the invitation from the Manager or by a strong recommendation from an existing Investor

The Fund collects deposits at bank accounts in Erste bank Romania or Revolut Bank (Lithuania). Accounts in EUR and USD are active.

Please contact the Manager about deposit details.

Contributions record

The Manager keeps a detailed record of all contributions, including dates, bank accounts, currencies and amounts.

Investors can at any moment request a statement document of their contributions and interest amounts accummulated hitherto.

Withdrawals

Investors can at any moment require partial or complete withdrawal of their contributions and interest from the Fund

Withdrawals will be done in form of personal transfers from the Manager before incorporation of the Fund, and as dividends after incorporation (check the tab "Legal and Strategy".

Reports

The Manager will consider organizing annual online meetings where detailed business report of the Fund shall be presented, if the Investors will demand so.

Interest rate

Annualized interest rate to Investors is 12%.

A 3% interest rate is accorded each 3 months, and compounding.

The Manager can discretionally accord sign-up bonuses or one-time bonuses following exceptionally good trading profits.

Cybersecurity

Alpha Centauri does not take safety in online spaces lightly. Hence, all devices used to log in into trading and banking services are secured with full versions of VPN and antivirus software. Subscriptions are paid annually from the profits and do not affect Investors
