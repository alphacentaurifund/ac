Future outlook and legal solutions

There are rules and legislation that are relevand to different development stages of the Fund. It is important to know and respect them to avoid clashes with governments, which are always stronger.

Intense learning phase (Jan 2023 - Dec 2024)

Current state of profits

All of the Alpha Centauri`s money is currently being traded in the personal name of the Manager. Taxes are being paid on profits by the Manager, 3% trading income tax + 10% obligatory tax for health insurance. Hence, all the money and profits in the are clean.

Status of investment/trading

In Romania, investment and trading activity is considered as independent activity, similar to renting out own real estate and authorised specialist businesses. No corporate structure is required for this, however, the investors must report the income on annually through a dedicated form and pay taxes depending on the realised income bracket.

Income tax - Romania

The Manager is based in Romania, where he pays taxes.

Since 2024, income tax for trading activity was reduced to 3% of profit per position profit.

Another 10% of total profit has to be paid to the national health system if the annual profit exceeds 6 minimum salaries.

The broker

The broker used by the Fund is XTB branch Romania. Natural persons and small companies cannot participate directly on the stock and commodity markets, due to costs and barriers to entry. Hence, a broker is a necessary intermediary which receives orders from clients and sends them further to the market hubs.

Investor Programme (started Aug 2024)

Deposits: legal considerations - Croatia

At this stage, the relationship between the Investor who lends the money and the Manager wko receives the money is resolved through spoken lending agreements. The principal is lended to the Manager, and upon withdrawal Investors receive the same principal amount and the accummulated interest in another transaction as gift, so no tax is owed. The Manager will provide more info upon request.

Deposits: legal considerations - Romania

At this stage, Investor deposits to Alpha Centauri are made through lending to the Manager. If the Investor insists on a formal relationship, the Romanian law item "Lending Contract (Contract de Imprumut)" shall be established. More info on this issue here.

Incorporation (estimated end-2026)

Next development step

When the running profit of the Fund will justify hiring an accounting professional, a limited liability company will be established and current Investors will be enrolled as constituting partners with their share proportional to the invested funds relative to the total size of the Fund. From there onwards, quarterly payments will be qualified as dividends.

Constituting shareholders

When starting a limited liability company, an expert will be called in to provide consultancy of legal qualification of the activity of the company and handling of the Investors` money in order for them to be able to withdraw the principal at a future point should they wish to do so

Government grabs from companies

In Romania, at this moment, the tax on profits for companies in financial sector is 16 %. The percentage may vary depending on the mood of the government. In any case this is not a significant increase from 13 % which the Manager pays now.

Dividend

A dividend distributed by a private company to its shareholders is a common and regulated issue. The company may make a quarterly assessment of its profits and decide on the dividend amount. Alpha Centauri will henceforth continue with renumeration of its Investors through dividends. Taxation of dividends is described in each country`s tax law. Intergovernmental agreements on avoiding of double taxation should prevent taxation of dividends two times, both in the country of origin and the country of recipients.
