Future outlook and legal solutions

Current state of profits

All of the Alpha Centauri`s money is currently being traded in the personal name of the Manager. Taxes are being paid on profits by the Manager, 3% trading income tax + 10% obligatory tax for health insurance. Hence, all the money and profits in the are clean.

Income tax - Romania

Alpha Centauri is based in Romania, where it pays taxes.

Since 2024, income tax for trading activity was reduced to 3% of profit per position profit.

Another 10% of total profit has to be paid to the national health system if the annual profit exceeds 6 minimum salaries.

Current mode on Investors renumeration

It is advisable that Investors re-invest their earned quarterly rate. The Manager keeps orderly record of all shareholders and their dues. Withdrawals of principals or quarterly rates can be done either through personal bank transfer from the Manager`s account, or through cash payments. Sums of money in question (less than 3000 EUR) are not supposed to trigger any additional taxation.

Next development step

When the running profit of the Fund will justify hiring an accounting professional, a limited liability company will be established and current Investors will be enrolled as constituting partners with their share proportional to the invested funds relative to the total size of the Fund. From there onwards, quarterly payments will be qualified as dividends.

Constituting shareholders

When starting a limited liability company, an expert will be called in to provide consultancy of legal qualification of the activity of the company and handling of the Investors` money in order for them to be able to withdraw the principal at a future point should they wish to do so

Endgame

A dividend distributed by a private company to its shareholders is a common and regulated issue. The company may make a quarterly assessment of its profits and decide on the dividend amount. Each investor should consult the legislation of his/her country regarding receiving dividends, but this is usually clear. Inter-state agreements between Romania and Croatia are in place to avoid excessive taxation of both countries.
